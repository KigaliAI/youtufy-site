What is YouTufy?

YouTufy is your smart dashboard for managing YouTube subscriptions. With YouTufy, you can:

  • See your full list of subscribed channels
  • Track subscriber & video statistics
  • Unsubscribe from inactive channels
  • Group channels with tags
  • Receive upload alerts from your favorite creators
